Diaphragm Seal Refurbishment

Professional refurbishment service to restore diaphragm seals to optimal condition and extend service life. When shridhan receives the transmitter with diaphragm seal system, the transmitter functionality is assessed by one of our engineers. A quotation for the repair of the diaphragm seal portion ONLY will be sent.

Request Consultation

In collaboration with PCI Instruments, Shridhan Automation provides a comprehensive Repair and Calibration Service for Pressure Transmitters with Diaphragm Seals. A repair can be a quick and cost-effective option. Most repairs can be completed and returned to the customer within 7 to 10 business days. This can save on the long delivery times and frustration associated with buying a new transmitter.

Typical Applications
  • Pressure, differential pressure, flow, and level measurement systems
  • Chemical, oil & gas, and pharmaceutical plants

Technical Specifications
  • Cleaning and repair of diaphragm assemblies
  • Reassembly and testing under operational conditions

Common Seal Damage Examples

Learn to identify damage types for timely refurbishment

Bulging indicates internal pressure issues. This seal requires immediate replacement to prevent system failure.

Complete seal failure due to overpressure. System must be shut down immediately for safety.

Physical damage from impact. Can compromise seal integrity over time and lead to leaks.

Rupture from material fatigue. Schedule refurbishment immediately to prevent complete system failure.
